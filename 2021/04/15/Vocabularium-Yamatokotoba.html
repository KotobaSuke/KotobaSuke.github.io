2021-04-15 Linguistica PV:

This is a vocabulary of Yamatokotoba (ヤマトコトバ) words.

Yamatokotoba is a conlang that replaces most foreign words (外来語) or Sino-Japanese words (漢語) with extant native words (和語) or created words by native morphology.
